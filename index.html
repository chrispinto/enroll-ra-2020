Running Start

Running Start allows high school juniors and seniors to attend SPSCC tuition-free and earn college credits. Each year, hundreds of Running Start grads transfer to a 4-year university in Washington as a junior with an associate degree.

Advantages:

  • Save $8,000 or more toward your bachelor’s degree.
  • Fee Waivers and Book Loans are available to qualifying students
  • Take classes not offered at your high school in a different learning environment.
  • Be more independent and create diverse friendships.
  • Set yourself up for a smoother transition into a 4-year university.

Make Running Start part of your success story.

High School Recent Graduates

SPSCC is a great choice for recent high school grads who want to transfer to a 4-year university or get into a trade career.

Advantages:

  • Save money on college tuition.
  • Attend classes in Olympia, Lacey, Yelm, or online.
  • Find your school-life balance with flexible online, evening, and weekend programs.
  • Find your place through student clubs, events, on-campus jobs, and more.
